Synopsis

Doctor Stranger tells the story of the life of Park Hoon (Lee Jong-suk). As a child, he and his father Park Cheol (Kim Sang-joong) were tricked and sent to North Korea. In North Korea, Park Hoon was trained to become a doctor by his father who was already a famous doctor.
